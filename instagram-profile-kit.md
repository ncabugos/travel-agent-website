# Elite Advisor Hub — Instagram Profile Kit

Everything you need to set up the launch profile. Copy-paste ready.

---

## 1. Account type
Set the account to **Business**, not Creator. You get the contact button, link tools, and insights, which fit a B2B SaaS better.

**Category:** Software (or "Internet Company" if Software isn't available)

---

## 2. Name field (the bold, searchable line)
Instagram lets this field be searched, so pack it with keywords, not a repeat of the handle. Max 30 characters.

**Recommended:**
> Elite Advisor Hub | Travel

Alternatives:
- `Elite Advisor Hub` (clean)
- `EAH | Advisor Websites` (keyword-forward)

---

## 3. Handle (username) options
Check availability in order. Keep it short and brandable.

1. `@eliteadvisorhub` (first choice, matches domain)
2. `@elite.advisor.hub`
3. `@eah.travel`
4. `@joinEAH`

---

## 4. Bio (150 character limit)
Instagram counts characters, and line breaks count too. Below are three options, all under 150. Pick one.

**Option A — feature-forward (recommended):**
```
Virtuoso-grade websites for luxury travel advisors.
Live in days, not months.
Built to your brand 👇 Book a demo
```
(133 characters)

**Option B — pain-point hook:**
```
Your clients fly first class. Your website shouldn't feel like coach.
Custom advisor sites, live in days 👇
```
(110 characters)

**Option C — clean and confident:**
```
The website platform for the top 1% of travel advisors.
Branded. Fast. Done-for-you content 👇
```
(96 characters)

Tip: the third line should point at the link. Keep an emoji arrow (👇) right before it.

---

## 5. Link in bio
Until the booking flow is final, point this at a single demo CTA.

- Primary: `https://eliteadvisorhub.com` (with the demo CTA above the fold)
- Or a direct booking link once live (Calendly, etc.)
- If you want multiple links later, use the native "links" feature (Instagram now allows up to 5) before reaching for a third-party link-in-bio tool.

**Link title (if using the multi-link feature):** "Book a demo"

---

## 6. Contact options (Business profile buttons)
- **Email button:** your inbound address (e.g., hello@eliteadvisorhub.com)
- **Action button:** "Book now" linked to the demo scheduler if you use one
- Skip the phone/address buttons unless you want inbound calls

---

## 7. Profile photo
Use the EAH logo mark, centered, on a solid background that survives the circle crop.

- Format: square, at least 320x320px, exports clean at small sizes
- Background: dark or cream to match the quiet-luxury identity, not pure white
- Test it at thumbnail size before locking it in. Wordmarks often turn to mush in the circle, so favor the mark or monogram over full text.

---

## 8. Story Highlights (the row under the bio)
Create these five covers in a consistent style. They act as a mini website.

1. **Start Here** — what EAH is, the 60-second pitch
2. **Tiers** — Starter through Agency, pricing snapshot
3. **Features** — supplier catalog, lead inbox, journal, portal
4. **Demos** — screen recordings of real advisor sites
5. **FAQ** — "Do I need to be technical?", "How fast is launch?", "Can I keep my domain?"

Cover style: same background as the profile photo, one simple line icon or word per cover.

---

## 9. Pinned posts
Pin **Post 1 (The Announcement)** to the top-left slot during launch. Once you have a customer site or testimonial, pin that as the second slot.

---

## 10. First-week checklist
- [ ] Handle secured and Business account enabled
- [ ] Name field, bio, and link set
- [ ] Profile photo uploaded and thumbnail-tested
- [ ] Email + "Book now" action button connected
- [ ] 6 launch posts queued in grid order
- [ ] 5 Highlights created with matching covers
- [ ] Post 1 pinned
- [ ] Bio link points to a working demo CTA
