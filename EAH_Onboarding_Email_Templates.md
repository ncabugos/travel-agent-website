# Elite Advisor Hub — Onboarding Email Templates

Seven branded emails that carry an advisor from first invitation to live site, plus one
variant for Custom/Agency tiers. Copy is ready to send — fill the merge fields and load
into Resend.

**Merge fields used:** `{{First Name}}` · `{{Brand Name}}` · `{{Tier}}` · `{{Portal Link}}` ·
`{{Intake Link}}` · `{{Preview Link}}` · `{{Consult Link}}` · `{{Site URL}}`

**Voice:** warm, personal, confident. The beta is invitation-only — every email should feel
hand-written, not automated. Send from Nick, not from a `no-reply` address.

**Branding:** lead with a simple header — EAH logo centered, a thin gold rule beneath it.
Close with the signature block at the bottom of this file. Keep one clear action per email.

## Email 1 — Beta Invitation

**Send when:** You're ready to invite one of the four advisors. Send from your personal
address, not a `no-reply`. The `[Personal opener]` slot is mandatory — this email is from a
friend who knows their work, not a campaign.

**Subject:** An invitation, {{First Name}} — a website for {{Brand Name}}, first look

**Preheader:** A Virtuoso-grade site built in days, and a founding offer I'd like to send you first.

**Body:**

Hi {{First Name}},

- `"We met at Virtuoso Travel Week and I keep coming back to your line about choosing clients instead of booking them."`

Here's what that means for {{Brand Name}}:
