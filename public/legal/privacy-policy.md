## 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

**Account and Identity Information.** When you register for or are invited to the Services, we collect your name, business name, email address, phone number (optional), professional credentials, profile photo (optional), business mailing address, and password (stored in hashed form).

**Billing and Payment Information.** We use Stripe, Inc. ("Stripe") as our third-party payment processor. When you subscribe to a paid plan, you provide your payment card or banking information directly to Stripe. We do **not** store full payment card numbers on our servers. We receive limited billing metadata from Stripe such as the last four digits of your card, card brand, billing ZIP, customer ID, subscription status, invoice history, and tax identifiers.

**Content You Provide.** Information you upload to or create within the Services, including journal articles, destination guides, hotel content, biographical information, marketing copy, photos, client-facing site customizations, and any communications you send to us or through the Services.

**Usage and Device Information.** Information automatically collected when you interact with the Services, including IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device identifiers, referring/exit pages, pages viewed, links clicked, session duration, and approximate location derived from IP address.

**Cookies and Similar Technologies.** We and our service providers use cookies, web beacons, local storage, and similar tracking technologies to operate, secure, and improve the Services. See Section 7 for details.

**Communications.** Records of correspondence when you contact us for support, sales, or other purposes, including the contents of those communications.

We do **not** knowingly collect sensitive personal information (such as government identifiers, biometric data, precise geolocation, health data, or financial account numbers beyond what Stripe collects on our behalf) and ask that you do not submit such information through the Services.

## 9. Your Privacy Rights — California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act ("CCPA/CPRA"), provides you with the rights described below. These rights are subject to verification of your identity and applicable exceptions.

### 9.1 Categories of Personal Information We Collect, Sources, and Purposes

In the preceding 12 months, we have collected the following categories of personal information as defined under the CCPA/CPRA:

- **Identifiers** (name, email, business address, IP address, account ID) — collected directly from you and automatically through Service usage; used to provide the Services, authenticate users, and communicate with you.
- **Customer records / commercial information** (subscription plan, billing history, transaction records) — collected from you and from Stripe; used for billing and account management.
- **Internet or other electronic network activity** (page views, click data, device data) — collected automatically; used for security, analytics, and Service improvement.
- **Geolocation data** (approximate, IP-based) — collected automatically; used for security, fraud prevention, and tax determination.
- **Professional or employment-related information** (job title, agency affiliation, credentials) — collected directly from you; used to verify Advisor eligibility and personalize the Services.
- **Inferences** drawn from the above (e.g., user preferences) — derived; used to improve the Services.

### 9.2 Sale or Sharing of Personal Information

In the preceding 12 months, we have **not** sold personal information for monetary consideration and we have **not** "shared" personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA. We do not knowingly sell or share personal information of consumers under 16 years of age.

### 9.3 Your Rights

Subject to verification, you have the right to:

- **Know / Access** — request the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the sources, the business purposes, and the categories of third parties to whom we disclose it.
- **Delete** — request deletion of personal information we have collected from you, subject to legal exceptions.
- **Correct** — request correction of inaccurate personal information.
- **Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing** — although we do not sell or share personal information as defined under the CCPA/CPRA, you may submit a request at any time and we will treat it as a confirmation that we will not begin doing so.
- **Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information** — we do not use or disclose sensitive personal information for purposes that require this right under the CCPA/CPRA.
- **Non-Discrimination** — we will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights.

### 9.4 How to Exercise Your Rights

Submit a request by emailing **[PRIVACY CONTACT EMAIL, e.g., privacy@eliteadvisorhub.com]** with the subject line "California Privacy Request." We may need to verify your identity, including by asking you to confirm information already on file. You may use an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf. The agent must provide proof of authorization, and we may require you to verify your identity directly.
